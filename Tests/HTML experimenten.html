Halfus Wyrmbreaker Raid Guide

Halfus Wyrmbreaker is the first boss in the Bastion of Twilight raid. This encounter requires coordination and understanding of the different mechanics and dragons that Halfus controls. Here's a detailed guide to help you and your raid team defeat him.

Abilities

  • Furious Roar: A knockdown effect that stuns all players for 3 seconds.
  • Berserk: Enrages after 6 minutes, increasing damage done significantly.
  • Malevolent Strikes: Stacks a healing debuff on the tank.

Drakes

Halfus Wyrmbreaker is accompanied by five drakes, three of which will be active each week. Releasing and killing these drakes provide various benefits and reduce the difficulty of the encounter. The drakes are:

  • Nether Scion: Reduces Halfus' attack speed and increases raid's attack speed.
  • Storm Rider: Reduces the cast time of Halfus' Shadow Nova.
  • Time Warden: Reduces the speed of Halfus' fireballs.
  • Orphaned Emerald Whelps: Reduces the damage of Halfus' fireballs.
  • Slate Dragon: Stuns Halfus occasionally.

Strategy

Phase 1: Release and Kill Drakes

  • Assign tanks to release and pick up the drakes one at a time.
  • Focus DPS on killing the released drakes quickly.
  • Healers should be prepared for heavy damage on the tanks due to Malevolent Strikes.

Phase 2: Burn Halfus

  • Once the drakes are down, focus all DPS on Halfus.
  • Be prepared to interrupt Shadow Nova cast by Halfus.
  • Avoid or mitigate the damage from Furious Roar.

Tips

  • Use Bloodlust/Heroism during Phase 2 to burn down Halfus quickly.
  • Coordinate cooldowns to handle Furious Roar and the healing debuff on tanks.
  • Communicate effectively to manage the drake releases and pickups.
