Valiona & Theralion

Overview

Valiona & Theralion are the second encounter in the Bastion of Twilight

They are two dragons that periodically switch bewteen phyical and twilight realms.

Phase 1: Engagement

The encouter starts with both dragons in the physical realm.

The raid must manage abilities from both dragons at the same time.

    Valiona's abilities

  • Blackout: creates a void zone on the floor (don't touch)
  • Devouring Flames: Targets a random player and spew flames on them.

    • Theralion's abilities

  • Dazzling Destruction: Targets random players with a fireball with splash damage
  • Engulfing magic: Applies a debuff that splits damage across team unless not stacked (1shot's player instead)

Phase 2: Twilight

At certain health thresholds, the dragons switch to the twilight realm.

Twilight Realm Mechanics

  • Deep Breath: Valiona marks areas for twilight flames, while Theralion performs an aerial assault.
  • Twilight Meteorite: Targets a player and leaves behind a damaging void zone upon impact.

Strategy

Move together as a group and use cooldowns when bloodlust is active, Stack up when someone gets Engulfing Magic or else you'd lose a party member
